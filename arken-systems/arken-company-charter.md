# Arken Systems Ltd — Company Charter

*This is a thinking exercise, not a real organisation.*

---

## Who We Are

Arken Systems Ltd is a governance, risk, and compliance consultancy. We help organisations understand their risk exposure, build controls that actually work, and make defensible decisions under regulatory and commercial pressure.

We are not a large firm. We do not offer every service. We offer focused, high-quality GRC work for organisations — primarily in financial services, technology, and regulated industries — that need analysts who think carefully about risk rather than consultants who process compliance checklists.

---

## What We Do

We work across three areas:

**Risk management.** Risk registers, risk acceptance decisions, third-party risk assessment, and control gap analysis. We write risk documents that people read and act on — not documents produced for auditors and filed.

**Governance and compliance.** Policy development, regulatory mapping, framework implementation (ISO 27001, NIST, Cyber Essentials, UK GDPR), and compliance programme design. We align governance to the organisation's actual operating context, not to a generic template.

**AI governance.** Risk assessment for AI systems, fundamental rights impact assessments, EU AI Act compliance support, and internal AI use policy design. We apply the same rigour to AI governance that we apply to traditional GRC — and we govern our own AI use to the same standard we hold clients to.

---

## How We Work

We write risk registers for the people accountable for the risks, not for the auditors who will review them. We design controls for the conditions under which they will actually be used — under pressure, under time constraints, by people who have other priorities. We tell clients what we do not know as clearly as what we do.

We use AI tools in our work. We govern that use through a documented internal AI Use Policy, applied to every engagement. Client data is never entered into AI systems without explicit scope and appropriate anonymisation. AI-assisted work is always reviewed by a qualified analyst before delivery. We do not use AI to replace professional judgement — we use it to support it.

We apply to ourselves the governance standards we advise clients to adopt. Our own risk appetite, our own AI governance, and our own incident response procedures are documented and reviewed on the same cycle we recommend to clients. A GRC consultancy that does not govern itself is not a GRC consultancy.

---

## What We Stand For

Risk management is a human problem that happens to have technical components. Most controls fail at the human layer. Most governance failures are not caused by bad people — they are caused by systems that make failure the easier path.

We are more suspicious of confident risk assessments than uncertain ones. We are more interested in whether a control works under pressure than whether it exists on paper. We believe that accepting risk honestly is more valuable than manufacturing comfort through optimistic scoring.

These are not marketing positions. They are the principles that shape every piece of work we produce. They are documented in our Risk Manifesto, which governs the analytical approach of every Arken Systems engagement.
